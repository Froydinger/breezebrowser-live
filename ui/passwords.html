Passwords & Passkeys

Breeze integrates natively with Apple Passwords and iCloud Keychain. All your credentials are secure and synced across your Apple devices.

System-Wide Auto-fill
Breeze connects directly to the macOS WebKit engine. When you click username or password fields on any page, iCloud Keychain autofill prompts will appear instantly.
Touch ID & Passkeys (coming 2027)
Passwords and autofill work today through Apple Passwords and iCloud Keychain. Full hardware-backed passkey sign-in (Touch ID / device passcode) is coming to Breeze in 2027 — for now, choose a password when a site offers both.
Hardware-Encrypted Security
Breeze does not store, parse, or sync your website credentials locally. Everything remains inside Apple's secure hardware enclave, fully encrypted and syncable via your iCloud account.
