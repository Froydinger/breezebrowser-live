How does Nav work?

Press ⌘E to open it. It's powered by Breeze Cloud through Breeze Cloud. Ask it to "summarize this page," open a site, look something up on the web, make an image, or set a reminder, and it drives the browser to get it done.

Use the AI on selected text

Open the assistant (⌘E), then highlight any text on the page. It appears as a chip above the input and becomes the focus of your next question. Hit the magnifier on the chip to web-search that exact text instead.

Search the web

Just ask — when a question needs live info, Nav opens your default search engine in a tab, reads the results, and answers with sources. Web browsing uses your normal browser; AI requests go through Breeze Cloud.

Set a reminder

Just tell the assistant: "remind me in 20 minutes to call mom." Breeze fires a native notification when it's time, and the reminder shows up under the Reminders tab where you can cancel it. (Notifications must be allowed — see Privacy above.)

Pin sites & make tab groups

Right-click any tab → Pin Tab to keep it as an app icon at the top of the sidebar (drag to reorder; size in Appearance). Right-click → Move to Group to file tabs into a named, collapsible group. Pins and groups persist even after you close the tab.

A site looks broken

Right-click the page → Fix This Site (Clear Data & Reload), or use the trash icon next to the bookmark star to clear that site's cache. Breeze blocks ads at the network level only, so it never hides real page content.

Privacy & your data

History, generated images, and saved passwords live on this Mac; passwords use your system keychain. Incognito tabs (⌘⇧N) keep nothing and wipe on close. Breeze does not store a provider credential in the app. When you ask Nav for help, the prompt and relevant page/tab/image context are sent through Breeze Cloud to a supported AI service, with daily fair-use limits.

Passwords & autofill work today via Apple Passwords and iCloud Keychain. Full passkey support is coming in 2027; for now, use a password where a site offers the choice.
