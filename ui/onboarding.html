Meet Tasks

Type / in Nav, a new tab, or the address bar and a menu of one-keystroke superpowers pops up. Pick one, add your prompt — like /research the best ebikes — and Nav does the rest.

/research
Reads several sources and writes a sourced summary page.
/summarize
A quick TL;DR of the page or video you're on.
/factcheck
Checks a claim against multiple sources.
/youtube
Creator Tools: title, thumbnail & packaging feedback.
