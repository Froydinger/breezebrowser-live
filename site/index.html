Local AI assistant

A genuinely private assistant built in. Summarize pages, ask anything, generate images, set reminders — running entirely on your machine. Nothing leaves your device.

Ad & tracker blocking

EasyList + tracking filters baked into the network layer of every tab. Ads never load, trackers never fire — no extensions, no setup, no slowdown.

Zero tracking

No telemetry. No profiles. History and passwords are encrypted on your device with your system keychain. Incognito leaves nothing behind.

Split screen

Two pages side by side with a draggable divider. Compare, reference, multitask — all in one window, exactly where you want it.

Tabs, organized

Pin sites as apps, file tabs into named groups, and let idle tabs sleep to save memory. A sidebar that stays calm no matter how busy you get.

Effortlessly fast

Built on Chromium with link pre-warming, smart suggestions, and picture-in-picture. Sites that already feel instant feel even faster.
