READ THIS FIRST
Breeze is currently unsigned while developer signing is being finalized.
To open Breeze on macOS:
1. Drag Breeze into your Applications folder.
2. Try to open Breeze.
3. If macOS blocks it, open System Settings.
4. Go to Privacy & Security.
5. Scroll down until you see a message that Breeze was blocked.
6. Click Allow Anyway.
7. Open Breeze again.
8. If prompted, click Open.
This is temporary until Breeze is signed and notarized with an Apple Developer account.
Breeze is open source:
https://github.com/Froydinger/breezebrowser-live
